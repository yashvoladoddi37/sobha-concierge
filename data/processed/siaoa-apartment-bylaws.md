CHAPTER-III
ADMINISTRATION

11) POWERS AND DUTIES OF ASSOCIATION:

The Association will have the responsibility of administration the maintenance of the Building, common areas and the annual maintenance etc., approving the Annual Budget, establishing and collecting monthly assessments and arranging for the management of the Condominium in an an efficient manner.

11.1) The Association shall ensure the proper maintenance of the Condominium in the usual course of occupation and enjoyment by the owners of the Apartments. However, the Association will not be responsible for any loss caused to the members they might have suffered by act of third parties orforce majeure. The Association is empowered to take appropriate action including recovery proceedings for any wilful damage caused to any of the common areas and common property-28*.

11.2) The Association is authorized to carry out any regular maintenance activities or reinstalling, if required, of any installed equipment or facilities. For any other new installations or new facilities whore capital expenditure up to Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs only) for normal contingency. In case of emergency, up to Rs. 4,00,000/- (Rupees Four Lakhs only) each contingency, the Association shall pass a resolution in AGM/SGM. In case of an emergency situation, SIAOA shall have the authority to install the same and get it ratified in the next AGM/SGM within three months-29*.

12) PLACE OF MEETINGS:

Meetings of the Association shall be held at a suitable place, convenient to the owners, in the Building.
